**1. 一个模型的输出，作为另一个模型的输入，需定义多个问题**

`model example1.mod;`

`model example2.mod`

`data example.dat;`

`option solver cplexamp;`

`problem P1: variables, objective, constraints;`

`solve P1;`

<!-- A is a param in model example2 -->

`let A := results from problem P1;`

`problem P2: var, obj, consts;`

`solve P2;`
